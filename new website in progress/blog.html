9th Post:

I'm getting the Samsung Galaxy Note20 Ultra! I'll be getting it this week, and I am very excited! It's the US version, meaning a Snapdragon 865+ for maximum and superior performance (compared to the international version with the Exynos 990 processor) with 512 gigabytes of UFS 3.1 storage, which is supposedly better than the S20 Ultra, which has UFS 3.0 storage. The total cost (excluding taxes) is $1450.

The specs of this phone amaze me. From what I've seen and heard from many credible YouTubers, the cameras are the biggest improvement over the S20 Ultra. While the S20 Ultra boasted specs that looked great on paper, it didn't really deliver them well. The 100x zoom was way too grainy, and the 8k recording had terrible focusing and image stabilization. However, this all changes with the Note20 Ultra. It can shoot at 8k 24fps that is actually usable, and has a 108 megapixel sensor. While this is similar to the S20 Ultra, it is delivered much better. Plus, you get a 4500 mAh battery and a 120hz adaptive screen refresh rate.
